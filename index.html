The Monsters Are Due On Pariah Artisan Circle

You are local police officers of a suburb of a City named Waterdeep. Late one evening you receive a call from a local who says that there was a large meteor that went by overhead, and the lights on the block go out. You and a couple of buddies go to investigate.

The call originated in the boonies where a couple of cul de sacks are located, each with 6 or seven houses in them. You are going simply to see what happened and who to send to fix the power issue.

The drive out there takes 30 minutes. Most of the residents of the neighborhoods have been there for a many years, Though they generally like to keep to themselves.
